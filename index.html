About SNART Foundation

Learn about our mission, vision, and the values that drive our work

SARAH NATHANIEL SNART FOUNDATION is a not-for-profit/non-governmental organization which came into existence in 2019 and was registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in June, 2021 (RC No: 159660).


Our foundation aims to advocate for human rights and social inclusion which contributes to creating a better social space that promotes the inclusion of vulnerable persons and groups in our community, particularly women, girls, youths and persons with disabilities (PWDs).


We work towards building their capacities, facilitating their inclusion in local governance and decision making, and reducing discrimination while creating opportunities for them to thrive.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To create a better every day for all and sundry, being able to truly inspire, encourage, build and encourage an all-inclusive social justice regardless of gender, ethnicity and disability.

Our Mission

To beam the light for our community and be a driving force that will promote and enhance the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) which is targeted at quality education and welfare marginalized groups such as girls, women and persons living with disabilities.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Dedication

Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives designed to protect rights and promote inclusion

Girls Rights Education Advancement Project (GREAP)

Dedicated to promoting the inclusion and rights of girls and women in society through mentorship programs, school clubs, and advocacy initiatives.

Menstrual Hygiene Management

Educating girls about menstrual health, distributing sanitary pads, and teaching production of eco-friendly reusable pads to combat period poverty.

Digital Skills & STEM Education

Providing Microsoft Suite training and promoting inclusion of girls and women in STEM education to bridge the digital gender gap.

Teens Corner Radio Show

A platform on Radio Maria 90.1 FM where teenagers host discussions on issues affecting them, amplifying youth voices for positive change.

GBV Awareness & Prevention

Creating awareness on gender-based violence, providing support services, and working to eliminate all forms of violence against women and girls.

Policy Advocacy

Advocating for the expansion of programs like Kaduna Digital Incubation Program and promoting inclusive policies at local and state levels.
